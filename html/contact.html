Contact & Support

Whether you need immediate help or just someone to talk to, support is available.

Emergency & Immediate Support

If you or your child is in danger, call emergency services immediately.

  • Police / Ambulance

    999 / 995

    For life-threatening emergencies and crimes.

  • Samaritans of Singapore (SOS)

    1-767

    24/7 emotional support for distress or suicidal thoughts.

  • National Care Hotline

    1800-202-6868

    Emotional support during difficult times (8am - 12am).

  • ComCare Call

    1800-222-0000

    For financial assistance enquiries (Mon-Fri, 8.30am-5pm).

  • AWARE Women's Helpline

    1800-777-5555

    Support for women facing crisis or violence (Mon-Fri, 10am-6pm).
