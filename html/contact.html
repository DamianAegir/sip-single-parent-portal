Emergency & immediate support

If you or your child is in danger, call the emergency services right away.

  • Police / SCDF

    999 (Police) · 995 (Ambulance/Fire)

    For crimes, violence, or life-threatening emergencies.

  • Samaritans of Singapore (SOS)

    1-767 (1-SOS) · 24-hour hotline

    For emotional support if you are feeling distressed, hopeless, or having thoughts of self-harm.

  • National Care Hotline

    1800-202-6868

    Emotional support and practical advice during difficult times.

  • ComCare Call

    1800-222-0000

    For financial assistance and social support enquiries.

For non-urgent support, you may also contact your nearest Family Service Centre. They can walk through options and applications with you.
